How to Make a Deck

1. Click the Membright link in the navigation bar and sign up for Membright
2. Once you sign in, navigate to the "My Decks" page or click this link
3. Click "Create a New Deck".
4. "Deck Name" should be the title of the video.
5. "Deck Image" is optional.
6. "Description" can be copy-pasted from the video description on Youtube.
7. "Source URL" can be left blank for now. I'll go in and set it myself after you create the deck.
8. Click "Save" and continue to make cards.

How to Make Cards

Continuing from step 8 in "How to Make a Deck":
1. "Card Type" should be set to "Question and Answer".
2. "Question" can be set to either a question, or something that indicates the answer, depending on the content.
Example:

Question: Where did agriculture develop?
Answer:...

This is a pretty straight forward question and answer card, but with something like:

Question: Advantages/Disadvantages of Agriculture:
Answer:...

The question isn't a "question", but you still have a good idea of what the answer is supposed to be.

3. "Answer" should be set to the information you want to remember.
4. Click the green "+" button
5. "Link 1 Label" needs to be set to the time of the video where this card is first relevant.

Example:
John Green first starts talking about where agriculture develped at time 3:08, so the Link Label should be "3:08"

6. "Link 1 URL" is obtained by going to the video on Youtube, clicking the share tab, clicking the "Start at" box and copying that link.
