What's With the Flashcards?

As you watch the video, the flashcards with the relevant content will open to help you follow along with the material. You can also click on the flashcards to jump to a specific time in the video.

YouTube is host to some of the best educational videos of all time. But sometimes these videos are so dense, it's hard for me to keep up. That's why I built this web app, to help me follow along, and then later, go through these flashcards in Membright (my brother's app) to make sure I don't forget the important details and facts.

This WebApp isn't worth much on it's own. To really extract the value of it, you would have to review the flashcards on a regular basis.

If you want to be able to use this flashcard deck, click here. Otherwise, just kick back, relax, and enjoy this great infotainment.
